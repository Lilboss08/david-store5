Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 2-5 business days within Lagos and major cities. Rural areas may take 5-7 business days. Express delivery is available for urgent orders.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with tags attached. Return shipping costs may apply for certain items.

Do you offer warranty on products?

Yes, all our products come with manufacturer warranty. Electronics typically have 1-2 year warranty, while fashion items have 6-month warranty against manufacturing defects.

How secure is my payment information?

Your payment information is fully secured with SSL encryption. We use industry-standard payment processors and never store your credit card details on our servers.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order status in real-time through our website.
